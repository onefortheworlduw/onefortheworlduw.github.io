Wisconsin Welcome Trivia Night

1/24/26

Location: East Campus Mall

Our team worked hard to plan a successful trivia night event with over 30 attendees. The trivia questions were closely related to global poverty, and many of the answers surprised the attendees. Did you know that training a guide dog costs around $30,000 - $60,000? That's a hefty price and not the most effective way to spend your money if you are trying to do good for the visually impaired. However, did you know that with only $1 you could help prevent blindness in the first place? Many children in impoverished regions are deficient in vitamin A, leading to blindness and eventually, a lower life span. One for the World partners with the Hellen Keller International's Vitamin A Supplementation Program to reduce Vitamin A deficiency, so by joining us, you could help prevent childhood blindness and learn more about effective giving.
