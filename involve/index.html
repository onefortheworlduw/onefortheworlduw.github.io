Get Involved

Have you ever saved someone's life before?

If not, One for the World at UW-Madison is the place for you! Surprisingly, saving a life is not difficult at all. We estimate that it costs only $4,500 to save one life through the most effective charities!

Some Statistics

Every day, over 13,100 children die due to preventable, poverty-related causes This is a big and overwhelming number to imagine and even conceptualize. To reduce this number, One for the World partners with four effective charities:

  1. Malaria Consortium Seasonal Malaria Chemoprevention Program

    2. This charity distributes preventive antimalarial drugs to children 3-59 months old to prevent illness and death from malaria during peak malaria transmission season, at $7 per treatment

  2. New Incentives

    3. New Incentives increases the uptake of routine immunizations through cash transfers, raising public awareness of the benefits of vaccination and reducing the frequency of vaccine stockouts, it costs around $17 to vaccinate a child

  3. Helen Keller International's Vitamin A Supplementation Program

    4. The Helen Keller Internationals Vitamin A Supplementation Program prevents childhood blindness and death at only $1 per Vitamin A supplement

  4. Against Malaria Foundation

    5. Against Malaria Fondation distributes and monitors antimalarial bed nets at a total cost of $2 per net

What YOU can do!

With some easy steps, you can be on your way to saving lives.

  1. Read through a short article about One for the World's partners and the charities that your donation will go to!
  2. Use One for the World's Impact Calculator so you may calculate your commitment based on the salary you will make in the future. Here's an important thing we want you to remember: you can commit now, but you can pay later, so you can change your commitment and when you commit however you want!
  3. Take the pledge Here, you can customize how your donations will be distributed throughout the evidence-based charities One for the World has chosen. You can also customize how much you donate!
  4. Join our chapter to meet like-minded people! Click on the "join now" button to sign up for our mailing list!
